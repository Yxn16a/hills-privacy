Account Deletion

Hills (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your Hills account.

Delete Your Account In-App

If you have access to the Hills app, you can delete your account directly:

  • Open the Hills app
  • Go to Account Settings
  • Select Delete Account

Once confirmed, your account and associated data will be permanently deleted, except where retention is required by law.

Delete Your Account Without App Access

If you no longer have access to the Hills app (for example, if you uninstalled it or changed devices), you can request account deletion by contacting us:

Email: support.hillssystems@gmail.com

Please include the phone number or email address associated with your Hills account so we can verify ownership before processing the deletion request.

Data Deletion

When an account deletion request is completed:

  • Your account information will be permanently deleted
  • Associated personal data will be removed from active systems
  • Some data may be retained only where required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes
